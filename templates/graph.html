HDL (High-Density Lipoprotein): Known as "good" cholesterol, helps remove cholesterol from the bloodstream.
LDL (Low-Density Lipoprotein): Known as "bad" cholesterol, can build up in arteries, leading to heart disease.
Total Cholesterol: A measure of the overall cholesterol in your blood, including HDL and LDL.
Importance: Monitoring and maintaining these levels are crucial to prevent cardiovascular issues and ensure overall well-being.
